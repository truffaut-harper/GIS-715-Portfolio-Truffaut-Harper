Map 1 - Black and White

This first map was designed to show the area immediately surrounding the John Heinz National Wildlife Refuge with a particular emphasis placed on water bodies and hiking trails. For this map no color was used.

Map 2 - Color

This map introduces color in order to show the transportation network in the wider region of Philadelphia, PA. Rather than the refuge being the focus this map shown the broader picture, potentially to aid in getting there.
