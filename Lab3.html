Map 1 - Pie Charts

This map uses pie charts to symbolize factors used in evaluating happiness by the World Happiness Index. Each section of the pie represents one of the predictors while the overall happiness is shown by the size of the chart. Data source: https://worldhappiness.report/ed/2018/

Map 2 - Graduated Symbols

This map uses graduated symbols to represent the number of stems present on various trees within the Denver Zoo.

Map 3 - Multiple Channels

This map shows the speed, direction, and temperature of various winds recorded within Brazil. Data source: https://www.kaggle.com/datasets/PROPPG-PPG/hourly-weather-surface-brazil-southeast-region?resource=download
